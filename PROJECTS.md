## Naming Rules

- Use lowercase kebab-case for new repository names.
- Keep one flagship repo per product line.
- Put research notes in `docs/research/`.
- Put business or launch notes in `docs/business/`.
- Put status updates in `docs/updates/YYYY-MM-DD-project-update.md`.
- Keep destructive, real-money, account-writing, or device-writing behavior disabled by default unless explicitly approved.
